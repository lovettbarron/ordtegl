## Description of Service

Ordtegl is a Danish vocabulary learning application designed to help users improve their Danish language skills. The app features:

- **3,500 vocabulary items** organized across CEFR levels A1 through B2
- **Spaced repetition learning** using the FSRS (Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler) algorithm to optimize retention
- **32 CEFR-aligned levels** providing structured progression through Danish vocabulary
- **Optional account synchronization** via Sign in with Apple, allowing users to sync their progress across devices

The app is provided as a learning tool for personal vocabulary development.
