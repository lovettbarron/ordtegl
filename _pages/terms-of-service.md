## Description of Service

Ordtegl is a Danish vocabulary and grammar learning application designed to help users prepare for Prove i Dansk 3 (PD3) and Prove i Dansk 2 (PD2) exams. The app features:

- **3,300+ vocabulary items** organized across CEFR levels A1 through B2
- **Spaced repetition learning** using the FSRS (Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler) algorithm to optimize retention
- **32 CEFR-aligned levels** providing structured progression through Danish vocabulary
- **Grammar lessons** with 40 topics and 414 interactive exercises (cloze, contrastive, and sentence reordering)
- **Camera OCR** for extracting Danish words from photos using on-device text recognition
- **Custom word lists** for adding personal vocabulary from camera, photo library, or text input
- **Exam-date pacing** that calculates daily study goals based on your target exam date
- **Automatic iCloud backup** of your learning progress

The app is fully local-only and requires no account, sign-in, or internet connection to use. It is provided as a free learning tool for personal vocabulary and grammar development.
