How It Works

1. Set Your Exam Date

Choose your target exam (PD3 or PD2) and your exam date. The app builds a personalized study pace just for you.

2. Study Every Day

Your daily dashboard tells you exactly what to do: learn new words, review old ones, or practice grammar. Sessions are short — perfect for the bus or a lunch break.

3. Watch Your Progress

Track your streaks, see your accuracy improve, and watch levels unlock as you master each stage. By exam day, you'll have covered the vocabulary you need.
